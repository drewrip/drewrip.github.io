About

I am a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy. For the year and half before starting my PhD I was a performance engineer at Red Hat where I worked on performance and scale for OpenShift. I did my undergrad at The Ohio State University, where I worked with Professor Yang Wang on distributed systems and databases.
